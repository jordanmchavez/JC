# Overview
4x turn-based strategy game with meta progression.
Key idea: 4x games are most fun early-mid game. Late game is a slog with huge armies, tons of cities, endless micromanagement. Solution: terminate the game once before it reaches the boring late game and restart with meta progression (new units, better stats, etc).
Key influences: Master of Magic (MoM), Eador: Genesis, Fall from Heaven II (Civ 4 mod), Battlegrounds: Fantasy Warfare (card-based wargame)
Player is a god reclaiming their realm after being overrun by demons.
Realm is a set of "shards", with each shards being its own plane. Visualize a shard as an island inside a planar bubble, totally separate from all other shards. Each shard has its own "flavor" (climate, native race, type of demons the player fights, etc).
The god cannot interact with their realms directly, they must send their demigod general to actually enter the shard and conquer it.
Gameplay consists of three layers:
- Universe: select which shard to conquer, view inter-shard resources, level up generals, meta progression.
- Shard: Hex-based map with turn-based 4x gameplay. Player builds cities, control armies, engages in battle.
- Battle: Turn-based battle of army vs army similar to the battle system of MoM/Eador/Endless Legend.

At start of campaign player creates/customizes their god with a point-buy system at campaign start, similar to MoM. Campaign consists of conquering the universe. During each campaign players unlock meta progression: more/upgrade units/generals, more creation points, more resources, better stats, better/stronger generals, etc.
